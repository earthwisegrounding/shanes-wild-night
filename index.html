🍺 Shane's Wild Night 🌭

Eat hotdogs. Drink beers. Dodge the cops.

How to Play

  • 🌭 Eat hotdogs to score points
  • 🍺 Beers give bonus points — but too many makes you drunk!
  • 🚔 Avoid the cops — they'll arrest you!
  • 🔥 Eat 5 hotdogs to go INVINCIBLE — Eminem joins you!
  • 💪 While invincible, eat the cops!

Controls

  • ⌨️ Arrow keys / WASD
  • 📱 On-screen D-pad (mobile)
